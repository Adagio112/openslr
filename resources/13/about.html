The RWCP Sound Scene Database in Real Acoustic Environments (RWCP-SSD) is a common database for research use that has been developed by the Real Acoustic Environments Working Group of the Real World Computing Partnership (RWCP). The data was recorded from 1998 to 2000.

You can cite the data using the following BibTeX entry:
